# FoldForge Blog & SEO Strategy

## 1. Goal
Drive high-intent traffic from MetaTrader traders and Prop Firm participants, converting them into FoldForge subscribers through educational, "viral-ready" content.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Prop Firm Traders:** Looking to pass challenges and protect funded accounts.
- **EA Developers:** Needing to validate strategies before deployment or sale.
- **Retail Algorithmic Traders:** Seeking institutional-grade tools to improve their edge.

## 3. Keyword Strategy (2026 Focus)
- **Primary Keywords:** MetaTrader risk management, EA stress testing, Prop firm drawdown protector, Monte Carlo simulation for trading, MT4/MT5 broker data sync.
- **Long-tail / Question Keywords:** "How to pass FTMO challenge with EAs", "Why backtests don't match live results", "Best risk management tool for MT5", "EA validation checklist 2026".

## 4. Content Pillars
1. **The "Prop Firm Guardian" Series:** Focus on protecting capital and passing challenges.
2. **The "Data Integrity" Series:** Why broker-specific data is the "missing link" in trading.
3. **The "Stress Test" Series:** Deep dives into Monte Carlo and Walk-Forward analysis.
4. **The "Aureus Prime" Case Studies:** Real-world results using FoldForge technology.

## 5. Viral Hooks & Distribution
- **Hooks:** "I tested 100 EAs and 95 failed this one test", "The $200k mistake most prop traders make", "Stop using 'Every Tick' - here's why your backtests are lying".
- **Distribution:** X (Twitter) threads, MQL5 forums, Reddit (r/Forex, r/Algotrading), TradingView ideas.

## 6. Blog Architecture (Technical)
- **Route:** `/blog` (Index) and `/blog/:slug` (Post).
- **Format:** Static TypeScript-based content for speed and SEO (no CMS overhead).
- **SEO:** Dynamic `<title>` and `<meta>` tags using a simple `SEO` component.
